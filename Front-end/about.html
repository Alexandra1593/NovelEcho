About us

What is NovelEcho?

NovelEcho is a smart book recommendation and reading tracker that helps users find their next great read based on selected keywords. Whether you're a casual reader or a dedicated bookworm, our platform offers a seamless way to discover, organize, and track your reading progress.

Why NovelEcho?

At NovelEcho, we believe reading should be an engaging and personalized experience. Our goal is to help readers explore new books effortlessly and maintain motivation throughout their literary adventures. Start your reading journey today with BookSeer!

Features

  • Personalized Book Recommendations Select a few keywords that interest you, and BookSeer will suggest books tailored to your preferences.
  • Reading Progress Tracker Keep track of books you're currently reading, finished, or wish to read.
  • Like & Review System Rate and review books to share your thoughts with the community.
  • Reading Reminders Set reminders to keep up with your reading goals.
  • Statistics & Insights View detailed insights, such as the number of books read, time spent reading, and your reading habits over time.
  • Responsive Web Experience Enjoy a clean and intuitive interface optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.
